Our Story

"Shaping Careers, Inspiring Confidence – SheWorks with Purpose"

SheWorks Vocational Training Program was launched in November 2024 with a mission to empower disadvantaged women by equipping them with practical skills for sustainable livelihoods. Recognizing the barriers many women face in accessing stable employment, SheWorks provides comprehensive hands-on training across multiple disciplines to enhance job readiness, foster entrepreneurship, and promote sustainable practices.

PARTNER WITH US

Training Programs Offered

Explore our hands-on training programs for skill development and growth.

Mushroom & Cultivation

This training empowers women with the skills to grow and cultivate mushrooms at home or on a small scale. Participants learn practical techniques for setup, maintenance, and harvesting—offering a sustainable source of income and nutrition.

Agriculture Training

Our Agriculture Training equips women with practical knowledge in sustainable farming, gardening, and crop management. Participants gain hands-on experience to grow their own food, improve livelihoods, and contribute to community food security.

Hair & Beauty Training

We offer practical Hair and Beauty Training designed especially for women, covering hairstyling, skincare, and makeup. Our program empowers women with real-world skills to build confidence and pursue careers in the beauty industry.

Jewlery Training

Our Jewelry Training program equips women with the skills to design, craft, and repair various types of jewelry. Through hands-on sessions, participants learn creative techniques and gain the confidence to start their own jewelry business or pursue a career in the field.

F&B & Housekeeping Training

This training program empowers women with essential skills in food & beverage service and professional housekeeping. Participants gain hands-on experience in hospitality standards, customer service, and hygiene practices—preparing them for careers in hotels, restaurants, or domestic work.

Crochet & Macrame Training

Our training program teaches women the art of crochet and macrame, turning threads into beautiful handmade products. Participants learn techniques to create home décor, accessories, and crafts—opening doors to creative expression and income-generating opportunities.

Pipe Cleaner Floral Art Training

This creative training program teaches women how to craft beautiful floral designs using pipe cleaners. It’s a fun, hands-on way to develop artistic skills and create unique decorative items for gifts, events, or small business opportunities.
